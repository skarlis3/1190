AI Transparency Statement

If you use GenAI at any stage of the writing process for an assignment, include a brief AI Transparency Statement at the end of your submission. Your Project 1 revised draft requires a detailed AI Statement of Use regardless of whether you used AI or not.

Format

Include the title "AI Transparency Statement" or "AI Statement of Use", underlined, and then a short statement of how you used GenAI in that assignment. Also include the specific AI tool used in your statement (e.g., "ChatGPT 4o" or "Claude Opus 4.5").

Example Formatting:

AI Transparency Statement

AI assistance was used to review sentence clarity and suggest more concise wording. I revised all suggestions myself and made final edits independently.

Some Statement Examples

Example 1:
ChatGPT was used to review sentence clarity and suggest more concise wording. I revised all suggestions myself and made final edits independently.

Example 2:
I used Claude to generate a brief outline of my ideas and to get feedback on paragraph transitions. All writing and analysis are my original work.

Example 3:
I used Grammarly Pro to proofread for grammar and clarity. No large sections of text were generated, and all ideas reflect my own thinking.

Example 4:
Microsoft Copilot helped me revise awkward phrasing and improve flow for my introduction and several of my paragraphs on pages 3 and 4. I made sure the voice and content stayed true to my original writing.

Example 5:
I used Semantic Scholar and Consensus to help locate scholarly articles on my topic. I read all articles myself and all citations come from the original sources, not AI summaries.

Example Statement for No GenAI Use

I did not use GenAI in any capacity on this draft.
