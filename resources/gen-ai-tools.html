GenAI Tools / Chatbots

  • Duck.ai — a privacy-focused way to use AI without tracking, built by DuckDuckGo. No account required.
  • Arena AI — free to use; compare multiple chatbots (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini) without creating an account. Note: this was originally a university research project (LMSys / Chatbot Arena) but has since split off into a company, so privacy policies and login requirements may change over time. See the FAQ for current details.
  • Claude — requires an account; often strong for academic writing tasks.
  • ChatGPT — may require login after a number of prompts.
  • Google Gemini — may require login after a number of prompts.
  • Microsoft Copilot — may require login after a number of prompts.
  • Perplexity — may require login/account after a number of prompts; typically better for search than long-form generation.

Reminder: Use GenAI ethically and in line with our course guidelines. See the GenAI Policy for details.
