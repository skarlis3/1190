Step 1: Analyzing Genre Samples

Grab a pen and read through the genre samples around the room. Look at the samples and look for similarities in:

  • How the samples are written
  • Tone and style
  • Format and structure
    • Like spacing, text alignment, and layout
  • Design
    • Like images, font, emoji use, etc.
  • Organization of information

Write down one or two of these conventions/similarities on the paper.

Step 2: Writing in Unfamiliar Genres

Each team will get one of these genres. Use the conventions (similarities) written down, along with your own deeper analysis, to write in this genre.

Write about the following scenario:

First year writing classes (i.e. classes like ENGL 1181, 1190, etc.) are starting to be taught by GenAI tools.

You can take the perspective of either of the following:

  • Option 1: This is already happening at your local college or university
  • Option 2: This has just been announced as a change at your local college/university

If time, you'll share these with other teams.
