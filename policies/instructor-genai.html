Instructor GenAI Use

In order to understand Generative AI and how it may (or may not) be effecting or even changing writing studies and adjacent activities, I've spent the last few years effectively just "playing" with it—seeing what it can do, what it can't, where it's helpful, where it's problematic or frustrating. As such, I've found some uses that seem helpful as part of my instructional work.

I'm continuously testing its abilities and limitations, but I'm also paying attention to how the technology is effecting my writing process, how I approach work problems, and where potential human limitations could be exacerbated by the technology.

In other words, I do not approach GenAI usage lightly. I'm trying to learn from it, poke and prod it, and explore the technology as fully as possible, particularly as it relates to writing studies and education.

Brainstorming, Writing & Revision

I may use GenAI to:

  • Help me edit and/or clarify my own writing (e.g., assignment instructions, presentation slides, resource pages, and/or feedback comments I've already written but think might be confusing or unclear).
  • Write example text (such as an example paragraph or example MLA/APA citations) to help demonstrate course concepts or to use as a starting place for an in-class activity.
  • Brainstorm in-class activities and assignments.

I will never use GenAI to grade your work or generate feedback on your work. Feedback, assessment, and suggestions will always come from me.

When I use GenAI to assist with any of the tasks above, I will always carefully check that the output still matches my original ideas, revise as necessary, and ensure the accuracy of any course materials. I also always aim to minimize GenAI revision/rewording in order to maintain my own writing style and voice.

Website Design & Coding

This website was designed with the assistance of GenAI (various tools over a period of time). Additionally, I use GenAI to write the HTML, CSS, and JS code for this site and individual pages.

Additionally, I periodically use Claude Code to run accessibility audits on my class websites and fix and/or improve accessibility for the sites and individual pages.
