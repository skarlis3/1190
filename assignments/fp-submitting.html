Submitting Your Portfolio

Your final portfolio has two parts, and each is submitted separately in Canvas. Both must be submitted by the due date — check the class calendar for the deadline.

What to Submit

  1. Research Paper (Final Draft) — Upload your final revised paper to the Final Portfolio: Research Paper assignment in Canvas as a Word document (.doc or .docx).
  2. Final Reflection — Upload or submit to the Final Portfolio: Final Reflection assignment in Canvas. How you submit depends on which format you chose:
    • PowerPoint or Google Slides: Upload as a .pptx file. If you used Google Slides, download it as a .pptx first (File > Download > Microsoft PowerPoint).
    • Google Site or other website: Submit the published link to your site. Make sure it's set to "not listed" (not private — I need to be able to view it). Your site must stay live through the end of the semester.

Double-Check Your Submissions

After submitting, go back into Canvas and verify that both items uploaded/submitted correctly. Open your uploaded files to make sure they're the right versions and that everything is viewable. Since this is the end of the semester, there is limited time to fix submission issues after the due date.

Late Policy

Late portfolios will not be accepted. If part of your portfolio is not submitted or is not accessible to me, you will not receive credit for that portion. See the Final Portfolio Grading page for how this affects your grade.
