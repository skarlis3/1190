Final Portfolio

Your final portfolio is your chance to show what you've learned and how you've grown as a writer and researcher this semester. It has two parts:

  1. Research Paper (Final Draft) — A final revision of your research paper
  2. Final Reflection — A reflection on your growth, strengths, and learning this semester

Both parts are submitted separately (see Submitting Your Portfolio for instructions), but the portfolio is graded as a whole. See the Final Portfolio Grading page for the full grading criteria.

Due Date

Check the class calendar for the due date. Late portfolios will not be accepted.
