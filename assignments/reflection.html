Reflection & Checklist

For this project, you will also complete a reflection and checklist to reflect on your writing and research process and evaluate your work. Complete this assignment once you have completed your revised draft.

Complete Before Your Conference

You must complete this reflection and checklist before your individual conference with me. We will use your responses as a starting point for discussing your revised draft for Project 1.

Get the Form

The form contains reflection questions and a checklist of revised draft requirements. You can get it from either link below — choose whichever is easier for you.

Option 1: Download from this site

  1. Download the Reflection & Checklist (.docx)
  2. Open the file in Microsoft Word to edit it.
  3. Save your completed form and upload it to the Reflection & Checklist assignment in Canvas.

Option 2: OneDrive

  1. Open the Reflection & Checklist on OneDrive.
  2. Go to File > Make a Copy to either download the file or save a copy to your own OneDrive to edit in Word Online.
  3. Once complete, save or download your copy as a .docx and upload it to the Reflection & Checklist assignment in Canvas.

Submission

Upload your completed form to the Reflection & Checklist assignment in Canvas as a Word document (.doc or .docx).

Grading

This assignment is graded on a credit/no credit basis and is worth 1 point as part of your Project 1 grade. You will receive full credit for completing the form thoughtfully and thoroughly. See the Project 1 Grade Breakdown for the full breakdown.
