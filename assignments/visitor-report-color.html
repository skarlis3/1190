The Historical Museum of Artificial Intelligence

Welcome to the Historical Museum of Artificial Intelligence. Today's exhibits explore how artificial intelligence transformed industries and professions between 2025 and 2050. Each exhibit has been curated by a specialist in the field and presents a unique perspective on this pivotal era.

We encourage you to take your time and visit every exhibit. Read the placards carefully, examine the artifacts, and don't forget to open the classified documents — you may find that the real story is more complicated than it appears.

Visitor Guidelines
  • This report is printed on paper — a writing medium once used in offices, schools, and libraries worldwide. As part of your visit, we invite you to experience this historical practice firsthand. Pens are available at the front desk.
  • Virtual reality recording devices are permitted in designated zones only.
  • Service androids must remain in standby mode during your visit.

The Museum Gift Shop is located near the exit. All proceeds support the preservation of analog artifacts.
